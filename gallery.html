About Me

My name is Michael Hommrich. I was born in Louisville, Kentucky. I graduated from Point Park University with a BA in Dance (Jazz). I performed professionally and even got the chance teach and choreograph while balancing various retail jobs on the side. That all led to Covid, which had a huge impact on the performing arts. Coming out of that I wanted to transition and try out some other strengths that I hadn't pursued in a while in my life, specifically coding. I began taking courses on Coursera (more on those on my Specialization page). Which eventually led me to the FastTrack Software Development course. In this course we have studied Java, SpringBoot, PostgreSQL, JavaScript, React and many other software that come along with that.
